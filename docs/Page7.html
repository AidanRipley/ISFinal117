2008 and 2009

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Yahoo makes a huge resurgence in which they now overtake Google after a 2 year hiatus at the top. Yahoo hits 9.3 billion visits in 2008 while Google only manages 8.7 billion visits. Facebook is now the 3rd most visited website hitting 2.4 billion in 2008. eBay is out of the top 11 being replaced by a familiar face in Yandex.

Yahoo now takes a commanding lead over Google outpacing them by almost 2 billion visits in 2009. Facebook and Youtube take a huge spike both growing by nearly 2 billion views while Facebook's only competition Myspace drops out of the top 11, being replaced by Baidu.
