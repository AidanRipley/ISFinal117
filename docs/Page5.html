2004 and 2005

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Yahoo still is atop the rankings with 6.5 billion visits, but Google has a huge spike, reaching 1.8 billion views and no signs of stopping. 2 newcomers in Wikipedia and Myspace both replacing CNet and About.com in their spots respectively.

It's clear that there's a new king in the rankings even if they aren't officially in first place yet. Yahoo goes down 600 million views in 2005 while Google increases 2.3 billion views in 2005. Myspace makes an impressive jump, from being the 10th most visited website in 2004 to 5th in 2005. Weather.com gets the boot from the top 11, being replaced by Yandex.
