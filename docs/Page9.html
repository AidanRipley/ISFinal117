2012 and 2013

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Yahoo continues to drop as it now sits below 10 billion at 9.1 billion while Youtube and Facebook eclipse the 10 billion mark being at 12.5 and 12.9 billion respectively. Google remains on top with 18.5 billion visits. Twitter makes it's first appearance in the top 11 as the only new addition taking out VK in the process.

Google becomes the first website to hit 20 billion visits in a year, clocking in at 21.4 billion views. Facebook eclipses 18 billion, a near 6 billion increase from 2012, and Youtube hits 17 billion. Yahoo dips to 7.5 billion, losing over 1.5 billion views in one year. Instagram hits the top 11, taking out AOL who finally leaves the top 11 after being in it since 1996 along with Yahoo and MSN/Live.com.
