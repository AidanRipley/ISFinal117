1998 and 1999

Written by: Aidan Ripley

At the end of 1998, it's still clear that AOL is dominant, rising up 200 million visits in one year. MSN overtakes Yahoo for 2nd place but just barely, out visiting the other site by 7 million. A lot of newcomers to the top 11 that include Amazon, American Greetings, Infospace, Real.com and Altavista. These websites replaced former top 10 in Geocities, Lycos, Infoseek, ZDNet, Webcrawler and Primenet.

By the time December 1999 comes around, people are freaking out about the world possibly ending but others are still searching on their computers. AOL increases by another 280 million visits, with MSN taking a commanding 2nd place, in which they out viewed Yahoo by 60 million in 1999 alone. 3 new websites hit the top 11 in eBay, About.com and CNET, replacing Real.com, Altavista and Netscape.
