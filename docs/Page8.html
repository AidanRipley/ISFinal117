2010 and 2011

Written by: Aidan Ripley

In a huge surprise, Google overtakes Yahoo in 2010 in which Yahoo stagnates in visits but Google gains nearly 4 billion visits in 2010 from 2009. For the 2nd time since 1996, there are no changes in the top 11 as no websites enter or drop out from 2009. This is also the first year in which all top 11 websites hit over 1 billion visits in that calendar year.

Google now proves that it's the future of the internet as it gains another 2.5 billion visits in 2011 from 2010. Yahoo still in 2nd place but loses over a billion visits from 2010 to 2011. Facebook and Youtube continue to dominate with Youtube gaining 3.2 billion visits from 2010 to 2011 and Facebook gaining 2.6 billion. The drop off from Facebook at 4 to Yandex at 5 is a staggering 6.4 billion visits. While it looks like there are two new websites in the top 11, it's truly only one, as MSN rebranded to Live.com. VK replaces Ask.com as the 11th spot in the top 11.
