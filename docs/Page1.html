1996 and 1997

Written by: Aidan Ripley

At the end of 1996, AOL proves that they are by far the most dominant website currently available with 146 million visits which is by far the most of any of the top 11 websites listed. Having over double the 2nd place which is Yahoo, it's clear that everyone who owned a computer used AOL as their main source to get email and read up on news. Yahoo comes in 2nd with nearly 62 million visits. Going to the 10th most viewed website is Primenet which is still in use but with only 1 million visits, not as much as AOL.

At the end of 1997, AOL increases by nearly double, reaching 286 million visits, still double of Yahoo and more than a lot of other websites combined on the top 11. Yahoo nearly doubles their total from last December, reaching 119 million visits, still good for 2nd place. From December 1996 to December 1997, there is one change in the top 11 websites, in which ZDNet is the 9th most visited website, replacing Compuserve in the top 11.
