2018 and 2019

Written by: Aidan Ripley

At the end of 2018, no big changes to the top 11. Baidu sees the biggest % gain going from 5.8 billion to 9.5 billion from 2017 to 2018. Facebook declines in visits from 28 billion to 27 billion in due to the scandal that is still currently going on. Yahoo is now in 7th place continuing to fall from the top it was at just years ago. There are no changes in the top 11 websites from 2017 to 2018.

Since the video was posted on October 2019, the visits counter only reads up to that date in which Google has an astounding 77 billion views year-to-date. Facebook sees more decline, now only at 23.6 billion visits. Baidu's one year spike was just that as Baidu is now only at 6.6 billion visits, 3 billion down from 2018. For the final year, there are no new websites in the top 11.
