2002 and 2003

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Yahoo just crushes everyone and everything. Increasing by nearly 2 billion views in 2002 alone, they now sit at 3.4 billion views, more than 2nd-4th place combined. MSN now overtakes AOL by 30 million but Yahoo still is 2 billion views ahead of MSN. Only one new website which is Weather.com who kicks out Lycos in doing so.

At this point, Yahoo might as well be the only website in existence, now reaching 5 billion views. However, Google hits 1 billion views being the 4th site to do so. AOL retakes 2nd place outpacing MSN by 200 million views. For the first time, no new website hits the top 11 visit counter and so no website also leaves the top 10 either.
