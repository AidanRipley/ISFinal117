About

Written By: Aidan Ripley
Source Video: Most Popular Websites 1006-2019

This website is supposed to be for a final project in which the website is discussing the most popular websites by visits from 1996 to 2019 annually in which the dates are end of the year or December of each year. Each page has 2 years per page to make this website most concise.

The Home webpage is supposed to give the viewer an appealing opening along with giving options of specific years that might interest the user. Also included on the front page are small cards of the 3 most popular websites at the time of the video being posted.

This About page is just to give a quick layout of the entire website from the Home page to the content pages The source of the information is linked in the about page so all credit is given where it is due.

The Contents page is the sitemap to the entire site in which it has the links to all the pages along with links to Home page and the About page.

The actual content pages have 2 years per page that have pictures of the visit counters at December of that year. Paired with that are quick descriptions of interesting facts that occurred that year such as new milestones, along with any new websites that have entered the top 11 in that year.
