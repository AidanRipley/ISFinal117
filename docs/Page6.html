2006 and 2007

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Google is now the leader and by 2 billion visits. All 3 of Yahoo, AOL and MSN decreased in views in 2006 but all still remain above 1 billion visits, in which Myspace joins them with 1.2 billion visits in 2006. Youtube makes it's first appearance in the top 11 replacing Yandex.

Yahoo doesn't lie down easily. An increase in 1.3 billion visits in 2007 outpaced Google by 500 million. AOL, MSN and Myspace all decrease in visits, and the reason for Myspace decreasing is a newcomer of Facebook, who replaces Walmart in the top 11 as the only newcomer to the top 10. There are now 8 websites that have over 1 billion views in 2007.
