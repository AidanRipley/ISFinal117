Google

Google is by far the most popular website of all time. Created in 1998, this website did not break the top 11 most visited websites until 2001 where it has stayed since reaching a max of 77.06 billion visits in 2019 up to October.

Youtube

Created in 2005, Youtube is the 2nd most visited website as of 2019. This website entered the top 11 websites in March 2006 where it has stayed since reaching a max of 28.37 billion views in April of 2019.

Facebook

Facebook is the 3rd most visited website as of 2019. Being created in 2004, Facebook did not enter the top 11 websites until June 2007 where it quickly overtook it's rival in Myspace, ending up with a peak of 28 billion visits in January of 2018.
