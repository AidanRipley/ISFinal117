2014 and 2015

Written by: Aidan Ripley

For the first time, there are 3 websites with over 20 billion visits in a year, but the more shocking thing is that the 4th most visited website only has 6.3 billion visits, which is 14 billion less than 3rd place. While Google, Facebook and Youtube continue to grow, Yahoo continues to fall as it loses another 1 billion in visits from 2013 to 2014. There are no changes to the top 11 from 2013 to 2014, but this is the first year in which all 11 websites have over 2 billion visits.

Google continues to make records becoming the first website to 30 billion visits in a year. In 2015, the gap between 3rd and 4th is even bigger at 19 billion visits, in which Yahoo continues to fall, losing another 700 million visits. For the 2nd year in a row, there are no changes to the top 11.
