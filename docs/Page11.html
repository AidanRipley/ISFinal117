2016 and 2017

Written by: Aidan Ripley

Google has another huge spike, gaining 14 billion visits from 2015 to 2016 as Google has 47.6 billion visits by the end of 2016. Yahoo continues to drop and is no longer the 4th most visited website as it gets overtaken by Wikipedia, who is still over 20 billion visits behind Youtube and Facebook. As in 2014 and 2015, there are no changes to the top 11 websites.

2017 is a year of big changes. By December 2017, Google has blasted past the 50 billion visits milestone and sits at 62 billion visits, while Facebook and Youtube stagnate at 28 billion visits. Baidu is now the 4th most visited site overtaking Yahoo and Wikipedia. There are 2 new entries into the top 11 websites in Pornhub and XVideos who take out Live.com and Amazon for the 10th and 11th spot respectively.
