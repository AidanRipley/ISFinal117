2000 and 2001

Written by: Aidan Ripley

In December 2000, the world has not ended but AOL's reign of terror might have. Only growing 60 million visits in 2000, Yahoo comes screaming into 2nd place with a total of 593 million visits and 370 million visits in 2000 alone. Only one website enters the top 11 which is Go.com replacing Infospace.

At the end of 2001, Yahoo is now supreme, crushing AOL on it's way to the top. After being at just 224 million visits in 1999, Yahoo is now at 1.6 billion visits, being the first website to reach 1 billion. AOL also barely reaches 1 billion but is showing signs of becoming outdated. 3 new websites reach the top 11 in Walmart, Ask.com and eventually supertitan Google. They replaced Excite, Go.com and American Greetings.
